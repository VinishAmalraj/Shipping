WHO WE ARE

At KING SHIPPING SERVICES PVT LTD, we are more than a logistics provider, we are a partner in your growth journey. Headquartered in Chennai, our operations are powered by a team of seasoned professionals committed to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

KING SHIPPING SERVICES PVT LTD, is one of the approved Custom House Agent with our own valid licence for clearance of Cargo at Chennai Sea and Air Ports. We are a Private Limited Company, registered under Companies Act 1956 in the year 1997 having Head Office at No 403, 4th floor, Wavoo Mansion Annexe, 48/39, Rajaji Salai, Chennai – 600 001 and branches in all Major Ports in India. We would like to offer our services in customs clearance of all your imports and exports through Chennai Sea and Air Ports and also in all other Indian Ports.
